# Your QDRN Radar — Owner's Guide 🫡

Hi! Your QDRN Radar is a little box that picks up the planes flying overhead and
shows them on a beautiful live map. **CaptainQ** will guide you through setup —
it only takes a few minutes, and you don't need to be techy.

## 1. Plug it in

Plug the radar into power (and make sure the antenna is connected). Give it a
minute to wake up. A little light will come on.

## 2. Get it on your WiFi

1. On your phone, open **WiFi settings**.
2. Connect to the network named **`QDRN-Radar-Setup`**.
3. A setup page pops up automatically (if not, open your browser).
4. Pick your home WiFi from the list and type your WiFi password. Tap **Connect**.

That's it — the radar is now online. 🎉

## 3. Finish setup with CaptainQ

Open your browser and go to **`radar.qdrn.io/md/setup`**.
Type the **PIN** that came with your device, then CaptainQ walks you through:

- **Location** — type your town so the map centers on you (we only keep it
  roughly — never your exact address).
- **FlightAware** — make a free account, share your data, and you'll earn a free
  **Enterprise** account! Paste your Feeder ID when CaptainQ asks.
- **FlightRadar24** — same idea: sign up, start sharing, and earn a free
  **Business** plan. Paste your sharing key.

You can skip the account steps and do them later — the radar still works great on
its own.

## 4. Watch the skies

Go to **`radar.qdrn.io/md`**. Tap any plane to see where it came from, where it's
going, what kind of aircraft it is, and how high/fast it's flying. The ☰ button
lists everything nearby; the ▦ button shows fun stats.

## Need to move it or change WiFi?

Just unplug and plug it back in somewhere new — the **`QDRN-Radar-Setup`** network
will appear again so you can reconnect. Easy.
